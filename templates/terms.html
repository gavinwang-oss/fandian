Fandian provides an AI-powered SMS concierge and staff task management platform for hotels. Hotel guests interact with the Service by sending text messages to a hotel's designated phone number. Hotel staff interact with the Service through a web-based admin dashboard.

2. SMS Messaging Program

Guests provide their phone number during the hotel booking process. By providing a phone number at booking, guests consent to receive transactional SMS messages related to their stay via the Fandian platform. Upon check-in, hotel staff register the guest in the system, triggering a welcome message. Subsequent messages are sent in response to guest requests or to provide service updates.

Message types include: welcome messages at check-in, AI-generated responses to guest requests, staff replies, and service confirmations.
